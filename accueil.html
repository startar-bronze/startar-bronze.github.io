Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is KharYsma Coins (KHACN)?

KharYsma Coins (KHACN) is an ERC20 token designed to revolutionize decentralized finance (DeFi). With advanced features like liquidity management, automated market making, and multi-platform integration, KHACN bridges the gap between traditional economic models and modern DeFi innovations.

When was NSTRB launched?

How can I participate in the NSTRB airdrop?

What is NSTRB?

The New StartarBronze (NSTRB), launched on January 8, 2025, is a BEP20 token designed to enable the migration of STRB tokens. It offers decentralized governance, maintains value parity with STRB, and supports community-driven projects.
